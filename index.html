About Me

"Renowned Bharatanatyam choreographer in the Panthanai Nallur style and expert in folk dance traditions, with over 8 years of teaching experience. Accomplished Nattuvanar and instructor of dance, music, and instruments at Yezhisai Geethalayam and other esteemed arts institutes. Co-founder and Managing Director of Yezhisai Geethalayam, Chief Trustee of Muthamizh Iyakkam, and Head of the Isai and Nadana Mandram departments. Founder and visionary behind Nayagi Costumes, dedicated to supporting the classical arts."
