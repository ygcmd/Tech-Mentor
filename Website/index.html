Welcome to Yezhisai Geethalayam (Conservatory of Music and Dance), Trichy, Tamil Nadu, India. We are a premier institution dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of traditional Carnatic music and Bharathanatyam dance. Founded with a vision to nurture talent and foster a deep appreciation for the arts, we offer a diverse range of courses designed to inspire and educate the next generation of artists.

Our Mission

At Yezhisai Geethalayam, our mission is to impart the highest quality education in music and dance, rooted in tradition and enriched by modern pedagogical approaches. We aim to cultivate skilled performers who can carry forward the legacy of these art forms with passion and integrity.
